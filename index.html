About Me

I am now an Associate Professor in the Department of Statistics at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My current research interests include statistical machine learning, high dimensional statistics, deep learning & generative learning theory and their applications in statistical and biomedical problems, etc.

  • Ph.D., Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, June 2011.

  • B.S., Sun Yat Sen University (SYSU), July 2007.
